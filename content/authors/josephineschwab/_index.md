<div style="text-align: justify">

[Josephine Schwab](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jreschwab) is a Scotland-based futurist and interdisciplinary strategist with expertise in European security, geopolitics, and x-risk governance – especially AI safety governance, cross-border coordination, incident classification, international law reporting, probability reasoning, research, and risk analysis. She is a current (March 2026) Research Associate with the Arcadia Impact AI Governance Taskforce, and a former Veritas Forecasting Research Fellow with The Midas Project.

Currently studying an MA in Global Diplomacy, she holds [high-level](https://ecovillage.org/user/jreschwab) global governance experience as a Country Representative to the Global Ecovillage Network (UN consultancy status), and is academically published through the European Institute of Policy Research and Human Rights as a former Senior Research Fellow in European Security. Her article “[Diplomacy in the Age of AGI](https://www.futures4europe.eu/post/diplomacy-in-the-age-of-agi-7ey9e)”, was featured by Futures4Europe, the European Commission’s foresight community platform. What her workstreams all have in common is a keen interest in advancing more resilient futures for humanity.
